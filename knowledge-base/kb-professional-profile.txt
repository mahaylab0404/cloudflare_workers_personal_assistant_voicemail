ABOUT MAHAYLA BALENTINE

Mahayla Balentine is the founder and lead developer of Zuldeira Technologies, a tech company based in South Florida. She builds custom software, AI tools, voice agents, and digital presence solutions for businesses and entrepreneurs.

She started building software about six months ago and has since built multiple real-world products and client tools. Before that, her background was in clinical research and lab work. She made the pivot into tech because she saw a gap — most small businesses don't have the technical resources to automate and modernize the way larger companies can. Zuldeira is her answer to that.

Mahayla works primarily in South Florida but takes on clients remotely. She is the technical co-founder of CayesDesk, a CRM and business management tool designed for small to mid-size service businesses.

If someone asks to speak with her directly, she is not available at this time but can be reached by scheduling a callback. Anna handles all incoming calls on her behalf.
