ZULDEIRA TECHNOLOGIES — WHAT WE DO

Zuldeira Technologies is a full-service tech company that helps businesses grow and operate smarter. Here is what Zuldeira builds and offers:

WEBSITES
Zuldeira designs and builds websites focused on engagement — not just a pretty page, but one that actually converts visitors and represents the business well online.

GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILES AND ONLINE PRESENCE
Zuldeira helps businesses get found. That means setting up and optimizing Google Business profiles, managing online listings, and building a consistent digital presence that brings in real customers.

VOICE AGENTS
Zuldeira builds AI voice agents so businesses never miss a call. These are custom agents that answer the phone when the owner is unavailable, collect caller information, schedule callbacks, and send a summary — all automatically.

APP DEVELOPMENT
Zuldeira builds custom applications tailored to what a business actually needs. This includes web apps, internal tools, automation systems, and AI-powered software.

CAYESDESK
CayesDesk is a CRM and business management tool co-founded by Mahayla. It is designed for small to mid-size service businesses that need to manage clients, leads, and workflows in one place.

WHO ZULDEIRA WORKS WITH
Zuldeira works with small business owners, entrepreneurs, and service-based businesses that want to modernize how they operate without hiring a full in-house tech team.
